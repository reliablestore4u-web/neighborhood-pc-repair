About Me

I started this journey because I genuinely enjoy helping people solve frustrating tech problems. For over 15 years, I’ve been the “go-to tech guy” for friends, family, and clients.

There’s no big storefront or corporate feel here. Just honest, reliable PC and laptop repair — right here in the neighborhood. I come to you, or offer convenient pickup and drop-off.

Whether it’s a virus, hardware issue, slow computer, or full system crash, I’ll get it sorted quickly and clearly explain what’s going on.

My goal is simple: make tech less stressful and keep your devices running smoothly.
